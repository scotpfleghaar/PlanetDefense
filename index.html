Briefing

DOC-01

1 — Auto turrets. Your base fires on the nearest threats by itself. You never aim.

2 — Research. Destroyed enemies fill the Research bar. Each fill pauses the assault and offers 3 upgrade cards — pick one to shape your run.

3 — Carriers. Glowing vermilion carriers drop a Core when destroyed. The Core falls to your base and banks permanent Salvage — but only if the base survives to collect it.

4 — Salvage Bay. Spend banked Salvage between runs on permanent upgrades that carry into every future run.

Goal: survive the waves and score as high as you can.
